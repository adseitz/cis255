Bring Something Into the Future That Doesn’t Exist Now

The class is a curriculum that has been developed and defined to encompass basic entrepreneurial skill sets that anyone can use: the ability to see and hear opportunity, take productive action, and create something new. The course grew from Terry Duperon’s experiences with invention and innovation within Duperon Corporation. It will help you identify your personal dreams and show you how to make them happen.
